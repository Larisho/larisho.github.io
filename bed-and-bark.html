The Bed and Bark



Check in: 4pm to 6pm
Check out: 9am to 11am

Our guests live with us as one of the family along with our 2 dogs, Bennet and Paddington. Couches, chairs, and ottomans are available for their comfort at all times. Sleeping arrangements are always discussed in advance, but we do encourage the pups to stay in rooms with the family.

We have lovely walking paths outside our front door and take daily walks to the harbor.

We require all guests to be up to date on current vaccines, parasite and flea/tick treatment. A copy of current immunizations from your vet must be kept in our files.

We do ask you provide food, leash, and harness for your dog. Please feel free to provide whatever else you feel your pup may need to feel as comfortable as possible while they stay with us.

We can administer medications, if needed, during your pup's visit.

During their stay, all guests wear a White Willow tag with our contact information.

We are fully insured.
