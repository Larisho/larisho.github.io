Boarding

Check in: 4pm to 6pm
Check out: 9am to 11am

Rate: $45 per night
Pickup/dropoff available for out-of-area clients in Freeport for an additional fee of $10

Our guests live with us as one of the family along with our dogs and dog-friendly cat. Couches, chairs, and ottomans are available for their comfort at all times. Sleeping arrangements are always discussed in advance, but we do encourage the pups to stay in rooms with the family.

We require all guests to be up to date on vaccinations, parasite treatment, and flea/tick treatment. A copy of current immunizations from your vet must be kept in our files.

We do ask you provide food, leash, and harness for your dog. Please feel free to provide whatever else you feel your pup may need to feel as comfortable as possible while they stay with us.

We can administer medications, if needed, during your pup's visit.
