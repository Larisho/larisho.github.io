Welcome to The White Willow Bed and Bark!



Nestled in a cozy, seaside New England town, the White Willow Bed and Bark is a family run business that offers home away from home care for your dog. Along with Paddington the Bichon and Bennet the Bassadore, your dog will be living as one of the family.

We are conveniently located 10 minutes from the North Shore Mall.

The White Willow Bed and Bark offers the best vacation experience for our 4 legged guests. Your dog will enjoy days filled with walks by the sea, playtime with the family dogs and comfy evenings in relaxing with us at home.
