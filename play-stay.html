Play & Stay



A couple of times a month we offer the Play and Stay package. The package includes door to door pick up and drop off, field trip, and a sleepover.

It's the perfect package for anyone who wants a day and/or evening out, worry free of getting home on time to feed or walk the pup! When your pup arrives home the next day, they will have had breakfast and a nice long walk. Happy pup, happy pup parent!
