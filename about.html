About Us



Having been in business for 7 years, we live on the North Shore of Boston with our Bichon and Bassador. We are a dog family - wherever life takes us we will never be without our dogs!

We started a dog walking and day care program after having a family member's puppy stay with us during the week until he was about a year. We haven't looked back since.

This is a true dream job!
