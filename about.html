About Us

Nestled in a cozy, seaside New England town, the Village Bed and Bark is a family run business that offers home away from home care for your dog. Along with our pups, your dog will be living as one of the family.

Located in Midcoast Maine, the Village Bed and Bark offers the best vacation experience for our 4 legged guests. Your dog will enjoy days filled with walks by the sea, playtime with the family dogs and comfy evenings relaxing by the fire.

We started over 10 years ago as a dog walking business and have grown into the successful B&B that we are today. We are fortunate to have clients that hail from all corners of New England. This is a true dream job!

We are fully insured by Pet Sitters Associates.
