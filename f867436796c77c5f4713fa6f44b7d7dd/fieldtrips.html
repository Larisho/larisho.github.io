Field Trips



A couple of times a week we offer Field Trips. We provide a fun filled day of hiking in the woods, walk on the beach, and/or strolling through one of the many scenic and quaint seaside villages mid-coast Maine has to offer.

It's a fabulous day out for your pup! They come home happy and tuckered out.

Up to date records from your veterinarian must be kept in our files at all times.

As with all of our services, arrangements must be made in advance.
