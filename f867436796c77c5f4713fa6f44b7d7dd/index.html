Welcome to The Village Bed and Bark!



Nestled in a cozy, seaside New England town, the Village Bed and Bark is a family run business that offers home away from home care for your dog. Along with our pups, your dog will be living as one of the family.

Located in mid-coast Maine, the Village Bed and Bark offers the best vacation experience for our 4 legged guests. Your dog will enjoy days filled with walks by the sea, playtime with the family dogs and comfy evenings in relaxing by the fire.
