About Us



We have been in business for 9 years and currently resides in Midcoast Maine. We are a dog family - wherever life takes us we will never be without our dogs!

We started, nearly a decade ago, as a dog walking business and have grown into the successful B&B that we are today. Our clients hail from all corners of New England.
