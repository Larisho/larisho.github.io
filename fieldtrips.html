Field Trips



Every week we offer Field Trips. With door to door service, we provide a fun filled day of hiking in the woods, runs on the beach and/or strolling through one of the many scenic and quaint seaside villages on the North Shore.

On warmer days, we offer day Field Trips to the White Mountains in New Hampshire and beautiful historical towns and villages throughout New England.

It's a fabulous day out for your pup! They come home happy and tuckered out.

Up to date records from your veterinarian must be kept in our files at all times.

All guests wear a B&B identification tag with our contact details during their stay.

As with all of our services, arrangements must be made in advance.
